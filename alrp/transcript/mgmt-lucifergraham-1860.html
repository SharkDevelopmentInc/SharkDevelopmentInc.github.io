- What is WorldHaven Studios?

WorldHaven Studios is a visionary design and immersive experience company dedicated to recreating the world’s most iconic cities and cultural landmarks with breathtaking accuracy and innovation. From the historic streets of Washington D.C. and London to the vibrant skylines of New York City and Paris, we don’t just build replicas we bring them to life. Each recreated city becomes a stage for highly detailed roleplay, educational exploration, and immersive simulation.

Whether for entertainment, training, tourism, or film production, our environments are more than structures they’re ecosystems of interaction Worldhaven Studios aims to bring nothing but the best in Development, Roleplay, and Community Interaction.
