* Being in the sniper unit is a valuable privilege that comes with significant responsibility and can be withdrawn at any time.
* It is never acceptable to kill anyone with a sniper rifle without justification.
* If you abuse the sniper or witness others doing so, report it immediately to the Tactical Administration Council
* Do not request permissions from the Tactical Administration Council or you will be denied.

How to use the Sniper Properly
* Position yourself on a building with a clear view of the criminal, and then wait for further instructions from Tactical Administration Council or Sniper in Charge to take the shot.
* If you are alone while on duty and need to use the sniper, use your best judgment and inform others of your actions to avoid any misunderstandings.

Things to Know
* Missing one shot means another insurgent alive and that means more people dead.
* The sniper is more lethal to shoot at a greater distance to what your shooting at. What happens is that the closer the enemy is, the less damage the bullets will do and this is due to the speed of the bullet.
* Before shooting you should try to predict the enemy's next move. So aline your self where you have a general area of the target.
